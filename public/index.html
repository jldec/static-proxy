Our Story

Future Media Concepts (FMC) delivers high-quality, accessible training for content creators, connecting them with industry experts through dynamic live courses and conferences. Our programs empower individuals and organizations to stay ahead of the curve, mastering cutting-edge technology to achieve their goals.

As your trusted source for consistent, top-tier training, exclusive certifications, and worldwide conferences, FMC offers expertise in video editing, sound design, desktop publishing, motion graphics, and IT services. With over 30 years of excellence, FMC has earned its reputation as a respected leader in the industry.

From renowned companies and educational institutions to freelance creators, FMC is the go-to choice for advancing skills and elevating careers.

Jeff Rothberg

President, Co-Founder

Jeff Rothberg
President & Co-Founder, FMC Training

Jeff Rothberg holds a BSC degree in Communications from Ohio University and is a 30 year veteran of the TV, film and video industries. He began his career working with the leading advertising agency of Kenyon and Eckhardt as Account Supervisor, managing such accounts as Quaker State, Pabst and Lincoln Mercury. He then joined forces with LRP Video, a leading 10,000 square foot post-production facility, where for in addition to aiding in the facilitys initial design and continual upgrades, Rothberg mastered a variety of disciplines ranging from facility management through production.

Jeff has a strong background in production, post-production and facility management. In 1994, he co-founded Future Media Concepts, Inc.
